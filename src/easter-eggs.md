### 2. Title screen: hold Z+X for 3 seconds
Trigger: hold both buttons for ~3 seconds on the title screen
Result: black screen fades in with:
```
✧ ⟁∅↺⇢≡~∴
witchblades.
bi-polar freestyle.
pink.
the maze knows your name.
```
Fades after 4 seconds. No other effect. Just a moment.

### 3. Rare death text (1/20 chance)
On death, 1 in 20 runs: instead of "the maze swallows you."
→ **"the maze buries you with all your gold on."**
Direct witchblades reference, disguised as dungeon flavour.

### 4. Gold door (secret room)
Trigger: reach floor 9 with Luck = 10 (max) on a non-boss floor
Result: hidden `state.secret` — gold background room:
```
✨ the gold door ✨
it was always here.
you just had enough
luck to see it.
अमर x ✧ ⟁∅↺⇢≡~∴
```
Reward: unlocks relic `[9]: "ice on"` — luck can't reach 0 from combat, only from bosses.

### 5. Win screen signature
If you complete the game: the standard win text includes `अमर` (immortal in Sanskrit).
Small. Easy to miss. There for the person who finishes.

### 6. Hidden relic: "ice on"
Name: `ice on`
Desc: `luck can't reach 0 from combat. only from bosses.`
Reference: witchblades — "when I die, bury me with all my ice on"
Unlocked only via gold door easter egg.
